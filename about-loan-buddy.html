Our Origins and Story

LoanBuddy is an innovative financial platform in Malaysia designed to offer comprehensive loan comparisons.

KreditAsia is a premier fintech company at the forefront of digital transformation in the Non-Banking Financial Institution industry across Asia. We offer advanced web-based applications and mobile apps, providing convenience and accessibility for our clients and their customers. Our comprehensive solutions encompass loan management systems, loan origination, credit management, and debt collection services.

LoanBuddy includes options from licensed moneylenders and aims to establish itself as a trusted financial authority through a targeted social media campaign that shares valuable insights on financial agencies, budgeting, and loan and credit card tips. The platform also ensures that any Islamic Personal Financing facilities offered are verified and approved by a Shariah adviser registered with the Securities Commission Malaysia, in accordance with the Shariah Advisory Council of Bank Negara Malaysia under Section 51 of the Central Bank of Malaysia Act 2009.
